About Game:

	Turkeys dominates Santa Clause Is a chaotic, holiday-themed platformer set in a mysterious castle. 
It features 4 players in total 2 local players sharing one keyboard + 2 bots competing for dominance. 

	The goal is to manipulate Santa Clauses by feeding them chickens to gain influence. By the end of the round, the player with the highest influence over the Santas wins the game! Get ready for a festive strategy battle filled with mayhem.

Game Play:

	Two players share the same keyboard to control their characters. One controls with wasd one with arrows. Its basic platformer.
